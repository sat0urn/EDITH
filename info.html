Some Information

Who would need this website?

The site may be needed for graduates of schools, colleges, for those who are going to enroll in higher education.And also for those who graduate from the bachelor's degree.

Why the site was created?

Based on our experience, choosing a university is not an easy task. As in our country there are a lot of different universities, but you need to find the right one very competently. That is why the site was developed specifically for school graduates, so that they could make a choice of the future educational institution. Many Kazakhstani universities have been published on the platform, and soon all universities in our country will be on the site. And also there are plans to add universities in other states.

What can be found on the site?

On the site you can find various information about universities in our country. Information such as: location, language of instruction, cost of a paid department, rating and much more.
