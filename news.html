University News

Persons who have graduation documents from a general secondary institution, vocational or technical institution, or a higher institution are admitted to universities in Kazakhstan. Applicants have the opportunity to study both at their own expense (paid education) and at the expense of public funds (receiving all kinds of grants). In addition to residents of the Republic of Kazakhstan, persons of Kazakh nationality who do not live in Kazakhstan, as well as persons without specific citizenship who live in the country, have the right to receive grants.

To enter educational institutions, in the Republic of Kazakhstan, you must pass the UNT (Unified National Testing) or CTA (Comprehensive Testing of the Applicant). CTA passes:

  1. graduates who did not pass the UNT due to the fact that they graduated from school before its introduction;
  2. those who graduated from vocational or technical schools;
  3. those who graduated from school abroad;
  4. those who graduated from school, where training was conducted in any language except Russian or Kazakh.

In order for a foreigner to enter the KZ-university, he needs to go through an interview. Some segments of the population receive quotas for higher education. Enrollment is carried out by the selection committee. For admission, you must collect a certain number of points.
