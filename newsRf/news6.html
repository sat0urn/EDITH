University news

Persons who have graduation documents from a general secondary institution, vocational or technical institution, or a higher institution are admitted to universities in Kazakhstan. Applicants have the opportunity to study both at their own expense (paid education) and at the expense of public funds (receiving all kinds of grants). In addition to residents of the Republic of Kazakhstan, persons of Kazakh nationality who do not live in Kazakhstan, as well as persons without specific citizenship who live in the country, have the right to receive grants.

To enter educational institutions, in the Republic of Kazakhstan, you must pass the UNT (Unified National Testing) or CTA (Comprehensive Testing of the Applicant). CTA passes:

  1. graduates who did not pass the UNT due to the fact that they graduated from school before its introduction;
  2. those who graduated from vocational or technical schools;
  3. those who graduated from school abroad;
  4. those who graduated from school, where training was conducted in any language except Russian or Kazakh.

In order for a foreigner to enter the KZ-university, he needs to go through an interview. Some segments of the population receive quotas for higher education. Enrollment is carried out by the selection committee. For admission, you must collect a certain number of points.

Kazakhstanis have applied for about 7,000 master's degrees

24 november 2021, 15:41
1476

Comprehensive testing for master's degree and entrance exams for doctoral studies will be held on December 2-11.

According to the results of the second admission, 6,778 applications were received for the master's degree. More than 700 applications have been submitted for doctoral studies, the press service of the Ministry of Education and Science reports.

It's worth noting that comprehensive testing for master's degree and entrance exams for doctoral studies will be held from 2 to 11 December.

According to the results of the TOEFL ITP test, doctoral students take an additional test for English language proficiency. It is held before passing the entrance exam. The total number of test tasks consists of 100 questions.

Additional English language testing is assessed as "passed" or "failed". You need to score at least 75 points to get a grade of "Passed". English proficiency tests, comprehensive testing and entrance exams are conducted in regional testing centers.

Applications for participation in the competition for educational grants are accepted until December 25, 2021. At the same time, winter admission to master's and doctoral programs will be based on the results of testing and entrance exams held from 2 to 11 December 2021. Admission to master's and doctoral programs will be held from December 26, 2021 to January 10, 2022.
